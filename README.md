# Problem Statement

Banks today face significant challenges in:

* Customer Acquisition
* Digital Adoption
* Digital Engagement

Traditional banking systems are reactive. Customers receive generic notifications, generic offers, and limited personalized guidance.

Most banking chatbots only respond when customers ask questions and fail to proactively assist customers in achieving their financial goals.

SBI requires a next-generation Agentic AI platform that can autonomously engage customers, improve digital adoption, and create personalized banking journeys.

---

# Solution

## YONO Sahayak

YONO Sahayak is a Multi-Agent AI Banking Operating System designed to transform banking from a reactive service into a proactive financial companion.

The platform continuously analyzes customer behavior, financial activities, life events, and banking patterns to provide intelligent recommendations and personalized financial journeys.

Instead of waiting for customers to ask questions, YONO Sahayak actively helps customers make better financial decisions.
